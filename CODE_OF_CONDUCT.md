## Code of Conduct

We aim for a respectful, inclusive space where everyone can contribute to the Koonj landing site.

### Be excellent

- Be kind, patient, and constructive in feedback.
- Assume good intent, especially around translation work or design choices.
- Raise concerns directly and calmlyâ€”issues are easier to resolve with facts and empathy.

### Inclusions

- Celebrate the bilingual nature of this project by avoiding assumptions about language ability.
- Use respectful language when referencing teams, regions, or cultures.
- Avoid off-topic signaling or personal attacks in issues or PRs.

### Consequences

Behavior that disrespects others, discriminates, or intentionally derails work may be reported to the maintainers. We will respond within 3 business days and reserve the right to limit participation if behavior does not improve.
